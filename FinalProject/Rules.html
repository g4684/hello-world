Rules of the Game!


  1. Select the Play Game menu item.
  2. Enter your name to play.
  3. Use your mouse to move the bottom-paddle along the bottom wall, left or right.
  4. You can also use your arrow keys to move the bottom-paddle.
  5. The key to staying alive is to make sure the ball hits the bottom-paddle and bounces back up to break through the bricks.
  6. You WIN when you have destroyed all of the bricks.
  7. If the ball hits the bottom wall before you destroy all of the bricks ..... GAME OVER
  8. You will be asked if you want to play again. You will need to enter your name again to play.
  9. When you are finished, click CANCEL to go back to the main menu.
  10. Your name and score will be displayed.
  11. You can view your ranking in the list of the top ten highest scores by selecting the "See Our High Scores" menu item.
